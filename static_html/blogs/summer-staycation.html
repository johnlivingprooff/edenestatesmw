Experience the Best Summer Getaway

Introduction

As summer unfolds in Malawi, there’s no better time to plan a refreshing escape to Eden Estates & Resort. Nestled just 7km from Lilongwe’s bustling city center, Eden Estates offers the perfect blend of luxury, tranquility, and adventure for travelers looking to unwind, explore, and make unforgettable memories.

Discover Unmatched Summer Accommodation

Eden Estates is renowned for its variety of luxurious accommodations that cater to all kinds of travelers. Whether you’re a solo adventurer, a couple seeking a romantic retreat, or a family looking for a fun-filled holiday, we have the perfect lodging option for you.
  • Continental-Styled Deluxe Rooms: Our deluxe rooms are ideal for those who seek comfort with a touch of elegance. These rooms are designed to offer a serene atmosphere, perfect for relaxing after a day of exploration.
  • Executive Studio-Loft Apartments: Perfect for longer stays, our studio-loft apartments provide all the comforts of home with the added luxury of hotel services. These accommodations are spacious, stylish, and equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay.
  • Two-Bedroom Family Cottages: Planning a family vacation? Our two-bedroom cottages offer the space and privacy you need, with plenty of room for everyone. These cottages are perfect for families looking to enjoy quality time together in a beautiful setting.

Summer Activities and Attractions

When you stay at Eden Estates, you’re not just booking a room—you’re unlocking a world of summer fun and adventure. Our resort is designed to provide a range of activities that cater to all ages and interests.
  • Our Lush Gardens: Take a leisurely stroll through our beautifully landscaped gardens, where you can enjoy the vibrant colors of summer blooms and the peaceful sounds of nature.
  • Relax by the Pool: Our sparkling swimming pool is the perfect place to cool off during the warm summer days. Whether you’re swimming laps or lounging poolside with a refreshing drink, our pool area offers a serene escape.
  • Family Fun at the Playpark: Families will love our dedicated playpark, where kids can run, play, and make new friends. It’s the perfect spot for children to burn off energy while parents relax nearby.
  • Dining at Oasis Café: No summer vacation is complete without delicious food. Visit our Oasis Café for a taste of local and international cuisine, served in a relaxed, natural setting. Whether you’re grabbing a quick snack or enjoying a leisurely meal, Oasis Café has something for everyone.

Experience Local Culture and Tourism

Eden Estates is more than just a place to stay—it’s a gateway to some of the best tourism experiences Malawi has to offer. From here, you can easily explore local attractions, cultural sites, and natural wonders.
  • Visit Lilongwe’s Markets: Immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting Lilongwe’s vibrant markets. Here, you can shop for handmade crafts, taste local delicacies, and meet friendly locals.
  • Explore Wildlife Sanctuaries: Take a day trip to nearby wildlife sanctuaries, where you can see Malawi’s incredible wildlife up close. It’s an experience that nature lovers won’t want to miss.
  • Discover Historical Sites: Malawi is rich in history, and there are plenty of historical sites to explore in and around Lilongwe. From ancient ruins to colonial architecture, there’s something for every history buff.

Plan Your Summer Staycation Today!

Don’t miss out on the best summer accommodation in Malawi. Whether you’re here for travel, tourism, or a relaxing getaway, Eden Estates & Resort offers everything you need for an unforgettable experience. Our rooms are booking up fast, so be sure to secure your spot today.
